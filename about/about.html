Our Mission

We strive to create a cleaner and healthier coastline in Goa by reducing waste, promoting sustainable practices, and involving local communities in conservation efforts.

Our Vision

A future where Goa's shores are free from pollution, biodiversity thrives, and people take pride in protecting their natural heritage.

Why It Matters

Goa's coastline is under severe threat from climate change, rising sea levels, and pollution. These factors damage biodiversity, affect local fishing communities, and erode natural landscapes. Through awareness campaigns and community action, we aim to protect and restore the coastal environment.
